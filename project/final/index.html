Welcome to SocialCue!

Thank you for taking interest in making new friends while gaining cultural awareness along the way!

What's our purpose?

To increase intercultural dialogue. We want to provide a way where people like you can form positive associations with many different cultures through positive personal experience.

Think of it as "walking in someone else's shoes" but from thousands of miles away! It's a change of perspective as you learn the difference and similarities between you and a stranger.

Basics

Pen Pals

Users will be setup with a complete stranger. There will be no filters to ensure that you are giving everyone a chance to form a meaningful conversation with you.

Pen pal conversations are mediated. What does that mean? Your conversations will have 'missions' or prompts that help guide you and "break the ice" over the course of 4 weeks.

Over time, these prompts will guide you towards a deeper conversation so that you really get to know and understand each other past surface level.

To ensure quality conversations and adequate attention, each user can only have four active mediated pen pals at a time. When your mediation period is over, you may add another pen pal.

Basics

Friends

After your 4 weeks with a penpal is up, you can choose to add them as a friend to keep in contact.

Conversations no longer have prompts.

News

The News section offers current events currated towards the people in your network.

Be up-to-date on news happening around your pen pals and friends and use them as conversation starters!

Enjoy!

Don't be afraid! Take this opportunity to meet awesome people from all around the world.
