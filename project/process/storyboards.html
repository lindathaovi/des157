Storyboards

With the initial 7 minute storyboard sketch, I outlined a very simple narrative of a person chatting with someone else around the world. I wanted to convey how convenient and easy the app is for anyone to use if they wanted to learn and open up their perspectives. It outlines the simplicity and convenience of messaging like one would with text messages or facebook message. After recieving feedback for a classmate, I focused on how someone would come across the app during my 10 minute storyboard sketch. Th setting was more defined in how someone with little knowledge about different cultures can learn to become more open and accepting. It illustrates how people can form positive relationships with others around the world.
