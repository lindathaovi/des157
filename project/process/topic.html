Topic Choice

My goal revolves around technology and peace, specifically the promotion of intercultural dialouge. I want to create a pen pal app that encourages an open and meaningful conversation across countries, cultures, and religions. My concept to is to go beyond superficial communication with prompts and games to facilitate a deeper understanding of a range of things from day to day life to the political and social atmosphere wherever their penpal is. I want to burst that comfortable bubble that many of us live in, only aware of our immediate surrondings. The app is meant to spread awareness and empathy to happenings around the world, beyond subjects that relate to ourselves.
