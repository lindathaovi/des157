Audience Research

After conducting research on who my target audience is, I have compiled information to form one persona that I would consider while designing the interactions for SocialCue (app name).

Jenn T.

University Student
Demographics
  • Age: 18-24
  • Gender: Female
  • Location: California, USA
  • Education: In Progress

Jen is a university student. She spends about 2-4 hours a day on her cell phone mostly for social media or for messaging other people. She considers her friend group diverse and does not consider race, culture or religion as a barrier. She has a curiosity to learn more about different cultures and people.She finds it more difficult to strike up a conversation with a stranger face to face than it is to talk to them through mobile. Jenn moderately keeps up with the news and current events.
